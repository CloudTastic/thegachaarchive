Hello peeps of the internet! This is "The Gacha Archive", a website where you can come to download things from Gacha Life, Gacha Club, and Gacha Life 2.
In the Gacha Life 2 section there is all of the static poses
When you want the backgrounds, assets, props, etc. just press the button and it will download them all, sorted by game.

Some of the assets, props, foregrounds, etc. will be traced by yours truely, so they may be a bit... strange, the lines could be wonkey.
When you want a pose you click on the box the pose is in and it will copy to your clipboard.
When you want an asset, prop, etc. you will also click on the same button, but it will download as a PSD, which you can use in "Photopea" to view, or just import into Ibis Paint.
When you want a background just click on the picture and it will download normally to your device.

Everything are a WIP, so some things don't work!
